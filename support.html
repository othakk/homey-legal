Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
You can sign up or log in directly from the home screen using Apple or Google. If you'd like to explore the app first, tap "Get Started" to browse as a guest, then create an account when you're ready.
How do I save a listing?
For long-term listings, swipe right to save it to your Saved tab, then apply for the apartment from there. For subleases, swiping right automatically sends your renter profile to the lister for review, and the listing appears in your Saved tab.
How do I list my apartment or sublease?
Tap "Get Started" and select that you're looking to list. Create an account, and from the Listings tab, tap "Add Listing" and choose between a long-term rental or sublease. Add photos, pricing, and details to publish your listing.
How do I contact a landlord?
After you apply, the lister will review your profile. If accepted, both parties will get access to each other's email addresses to get in contact.
How do I change my campus?
Go to the Discover tab and tap the filter icon. Select your university from the campus dropdown to see listings near your school. Homey supports 300+ campuses across the US.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile and press "Delete Account." This will permanently remove all your personal data, saved listings, and account information. This action cannot be undone.
